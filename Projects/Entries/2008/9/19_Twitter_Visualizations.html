Twitter Visualizations

 

Extracted Twitter data to visually represent characteristics of users & better understand social networking. Used Java, SQL, Python, and Processing to create visualizations of the daily rhythms of micro-blog postings by utilizing Voronoi Diagrams and Delaunay Triangulations Contributed to the paper presented at Siggraph 2010

 

Fall 2008

Alex Dragulescu and Judith Donath

Sociable Media Group- MIT Media Lab
